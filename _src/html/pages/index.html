What's new at Aqovia

New office in Hoofddorp, Netherlands

Aqovia UK Limited announced the opening of their new office in Hoofddorp, the Netherlands. This new location reinforces Aqovia’s international presence and operationally better serves its strategic offerings, such as the Aqovia A1 Autonomic Platform.

“We are delighted to announce the opening of our new Dutch office”, said Robert Wolters, International Sales Manager at Aqovia.” This new office is in response to the growing demand for our offerings and is located near some of world’s leading companies.  It is a strategic investment that shows our continuous commitment to the European and International market”, Wolters added.

“It will be our pleasure to meet new and existing customers and serve them through our new Dutch office”.
