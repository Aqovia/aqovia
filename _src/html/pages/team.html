The team at Aqovia

The Aqovia team are made up of experts in digital innovation and emerging technologies, based in London and the Netherlands.

We are business, system and data analysts, software developers, data scientists, user researchers, project and delivery managers and consultants.

Executive leadership

Muhammad Malik

CEO

Muhammad Malik is CEO and founder of Aqovia.

Muhammad’s professional career started at Peoples Bank in late 1998. Muhammad joined U.S. telecommunications provider Sprint and in 2001 he was transferred from the U.S. to London, to act as Head of Network Planning and Engineering for Sprint Europe, Mid-East and Africa.

After leading several large scale multi-year telecommunication projects (more than $250 million annual budget) in early 2008, Muhammad founded a joint venture with Cable&Wireless (C&W) and newly founded Aqovia.

Muhammad is currently focused on several applied innovation and infrastructure ventures, including:

  • financial inclusion and banking
  • a portfolio of new energy infrastructure plants, such as waste to energy

He received his bachelors in Business Administration and in Management Information Systems from Ottawa University.
