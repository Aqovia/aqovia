The team at Aqovia

The Aqovia team are made up of experts in digital innovation and emerging technologies, based in London and the Netherlands.

We are business, system and data analysts, software developers, data scientists, user researchers, project and delivery managers and consultants.

Executive leadership

Muhammad Malik

CEO

Muhammad Malik is CEO and founder of Aqovia.

Muhammad’s professional career started at Peoples Bank US in late 1998. Muhammad joined U.S. telecommunications provider Sprint in 2001 and transferred from the U.S. to London, as Head of Network Planning and Engineering for Sprint Europe, Mid-East and Africa.

After leading several large scale multi-year telecommunication projects at Sprint and in early 2008, Muhammad founded a joint venture with Cable&Wireless (C&W) and newly founded Aqovia.

Muhammad is currently focused on several applied innovation, social responsibility and new infrastructure ventures, including:

  • Banking
  • New energy infrastructure plants, such as energy from waste

He received his bachelors in Business Administration and in Management Information Systems from Ottawa University.

Our specialised skills include:

  • Software development
  • Artificial Intelligence
  • Microservices
  • Cloud Storage
  • Smart contracts
  • Distributed ledger technology

Not only are our team filled with in-depth industry knowledge and digital expertise but are focused on collaborative working, ensuring that you - our client - get the bespoke service you require.
