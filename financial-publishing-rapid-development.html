Financial Publishing

Financial research and publishing house with $500m+ revenues and a presence across leading financial hubs around the world

1. What

Develop and launch a minimal viable product leveraging semantic web technologies in three months.

2. Why

  • Increase revenues and efficiency of content creation
  • Demonstrate thought leadership and enhance the customer experience

3. How

Aqovia deployed a specialist MVP team to successfully develop and deliver the B2C product on time. Core deliverables included:

  • Rapid analysis and prioritisation of top features and story backlog using Aqovia’s “MVP Feature Blueprint” approach.
  • Layered wireframes and interactive prototypes
  • Continual customer feedback loops to refine and validate features
  • Process change management to align existing business e.g. content management, training and support.

4. Value

  • Target $10m+ sales uplift from new interactive services
  • Interactive data analysis - unlocking value from historical content
  • User generated research - allowing users to pull content based on interests.
  • Alpha product launched, on time to tenant customers.
